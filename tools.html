Text-to-Video

Tools like OpenAI's Sora and Google Veo generate short videos from text prompts using diffusion and transformer-based systems. They simulate motion, lighting, and physics.

Image generation models create high-resolution visuals from prompts. They rely on latent diffusion models trained on large datasets.
