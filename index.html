Phase 2: Google Nano Banana 2 vs. OpenAI DALL-E 3

Introduction

This research evaluates the evolution of AI image generation. We are comparing Google's Nano Banana 2 and OpenAI's DALL-E 3 to see how different wording (Prompt Engineering) affects the final quality and lighting of the images.

AI PROMPT TEST

Prompt: "A futuristic city"

OpenAI DALL-E 3

Test 02: Structural Detail

Google Nano Banana 2
OpenAI DALL-E 3

Analysis: Nano Banana 2 handled the neon light glow better than DALL-E.

Test 03: Professional Fidelity

Prompt: "A cyberpunk city... rainy streets... 4K..."

Google Nano Banana 2
OpenAI DALL-E 3

Analysis: Google's reflections look more realistic, but DALL-E's holograms are clearer.
