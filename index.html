Overview

This newsletter explores modern AI creativity tools including text-to-video, text-to-image, and generative media systems. The focus is on how these systems work, how they differ, and their real-world potential.

What I Learned

AI creative tools use diffusion models, transformer architectures, and large training datasets to generate media from prompts. Each platform varies in model size, output control, realism, and commercial accessibility.
