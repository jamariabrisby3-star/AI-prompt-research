Evaluation Case 02: Environmental Detail & Illumination
"A futuristic city at night with neon lights, flying cars, and tall glass skyscrapers"
Google: Nano Banana 2
OpenAI: DALL-E 3

Deep Contrast Test: Atmospheric Depth & Latent Adherence

Adding atmospheric constraints (night and neon) triggered a superior response in the Google engine. Nano Banana 2 effectively simulated light bloom and atmospheric haze, anchoring the elements within a coherent environment. Critically, **DALL-E 3 failed to maintain latent consistency**, generating a new structural composition, while the Google model demonstrated true adherence by modifying the base architecture established in Test 1.
