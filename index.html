3-Prompt Test – Google Nano Banana 2

We tested how changes in prompt wording affect the generated images using Google Nano Banana 2. Each prompt produced a unique image, showing how AI interprets specificity and added details.

Prompt 1: A futuristic city

Prompt 2: A futuristic city at night with neon lights, flying cars, and tall glass skyscrapers

Prompt 3: A cyberpunk futuristic city at night, neon pink and blue lighting, flying cars in the sky, holographic advertisements, rainy streets reflecting light, ultra-detailed, cinematic lighting, 4K, wide angle

Observations: Small changes in wording dramatically altered the images. Increasing specificity added more details, lighting effects, and cinematic style, demonstrating the importance of precise prompt crafting for desired AI outputs.
